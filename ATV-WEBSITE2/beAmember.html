Take Note!

  •              Are you excited to spend time with your family and friends on enjoyable ATV rides? If so, you need to take note of the following:

  • 1. Account: First, you need to create an account on the ATV Rental Website                     so you can reserve the ATVs and tour guides for your preferred                     day and time.

  • 2. Android App: If you already have an account, you can download the                     application to make reservations for the ATVs, and it is also                     required for the ATV rental website itself.

  • 3. Things to Bring: Once you have made your reservation, make sure to                     bring the following items: a pair of trekking shoes,                     slippers, sunblock and insect repellent, a small backpack,                     hydration pack, swimming gear, camera, light snack, change of                     clothes, and the spirit of adventure!

  • 4. Follow the Rules and Regulations: While at our ATV Rental, it is essential                      to follow the rules and regulations for your safety. You will be                      assigned a tour guide to ensure a better understanding of the                      necessary guidelines.

  • 5. Praying: Keep praying, seek guidance from the Lord, praise Him daily, and                     don't forget to be thankful for the new day and the blessings                     that made you happy today.

Rules and Regulation

  • Safety Gear: All riders are required to wear appropriate safety gear, including helmets, gloves, and goggles, throughout the entire duration of the ATV ride.

  • Age Limit: Only individuals of legal age and possessing a valid driver's license or those under the supervision of a legal guardian are allowed to operate the ATV.

  • No Alcohol or Drugs: Riding under the influence of alcohol or drugs is strictly prohibited. Any rider suspected of being impaired will not be allowed to participate.

  • Follow the Guide: Riders must follow the designated ATV guide at all times during the tour. Instructions given by the guide must be followed promptly.

  • Stay on Designated Trails: It is prohibited to venture off the designated ATV trails to protect the local flora and fauna and ensure the safety of the riders.

  • One Rider per ATV: Each ATV is designed for a single rider. Riding with a passenger is not allowed, except in ATVs specifically designed for two persons.

  • Maintain Safe Distance: Riders must maintain a safe distance from the ATV ahead of them to avoid collisions.

  • No Racing or Stunts: Racing, reckless driving, or performing stunts with the ATVs is strictly prohibited.

  • Environmental Responsibility: All riders must respect the environment and not leave any trash or litter during the tour.

  • Assumption of Risk: Riders must sign a liability waiver and assume responsibility for any risks associated with ATV riding before participating in the activity.
